Maitree didn't begin in a classroom or an office. It began with one visit, one notebook, and one heart full of hope.

In 2024, Aashma, a student from Sunsari, visited an orphanage in Kathmandu. She didn't go as part of an organization‚Äîshe went as a friend. What started as quiet weekly visits soon turned into bright moments of learning, storytelling, poetry, and joy.

Later that year, she officially founded Maitree in Itahari, determined to build something long-lasting and meaningful.

The word Maitree means friendship‚Äîand that's what we offer: real friendships that nurture learning, healing, and growth.
